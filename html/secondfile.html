Mandi is a traditional Yemeni dish featuring slow-cooked, tender meat (lamb or chicken) and fragrant basmati rice, flavored with spices like cardamom, cloves, and saffron. It has a distinct smoky, mild flavor from being cooked in a pit (tandoor) and is traditionally served on a large platter with special dips.

Taste Profile: Mild, savory, and smoky with delicate spice notes. The meat is incredibly tender from slow-cooking.
